A Little About Me

Welcome to Rosemary Boards! My name is Mariangela, and I’m passionate about crafting charcuterie boards and grazing tables that are as beautiful as they are delicious. Based in Dublin, Ireland, I focus on using locally sourced Irish ingredients to create unique and customisable options for every occasion—whether it’s a small gathering, a wedding, or a corporate event. If you’re looking for luxury grazing tables or the perfect charcuterie board for your next event, I’d love to help make your occasion special. Get in touch today to explore the possibilities and discover the art of grazing with Rosemary Boards.
